Grid System

Grid is crucial in responsive Web development. It gives many oppurtunities to layout website in many different ways and fast, without worring styling.

Easy-Kit grid is devided to 5 different categories:

  • Large(< 992px),
  • Medium(< 768px),
  • Small(< 576px),
  • Extra Small(< 400px)
  • and Default(non-responsive).

It's a good way to control layout sizes in different types of screens(Desktops, mobiles etc).

Grid example(try to resize your screen to see effects):


Code:

<div class="grid">
	<div class="col-6-small border">
		Col-6-Small
	</div>
	<div class="col-6-small border">
		Col-6-Small
	</div>
</div>

Result:

Col-6-Small
Col-6-Small

Code:

<div class="grid">
	<div class="col-6-small col-10-medium border">
		Col-6-small Col-10-medium
	</div>
	<div class="col-6-small col-2-medium border">
		Col-6-small Col-2-medium
	</div>
</div>

Result:

Col-6-small Col-10-medium
Col-6-small Col-2-medium

Typography

Headers

Headers are very common things that sites use, so it's important to keep them as good as possible. One of the problems, even I(eroxeN) had a problem, when headers didn't fit in mobile. Now there is no problem with that.

Code:

<div class="grid">
	<div class="col-6-medium">
		<h1>Header1</h1>
		<h2>Header2</h2>
		<h3>Header3</h3>
	</div>
	<div class="col-6-medium">
		<h4>Header4</h4>
		<h5>Header5</h5>
		<h6>Header6</h6>
	</div>
</div>
